1. June 4th

    Saw me a new crew of takers come out The Pike today, at least as I hadn’t seen them before. Decided to use one of my drones to follow them; they had a Dronkey with them, so figured they might know the location of a sizable score.

    Turns out, either they’s a few hammers short of a full toolbox or they got themselves balls the size of Ubiq satellites, because they done just about they craziest thing I ever saw. You see, yesterday a PunchBot got itself caught inside the old Macy’s on 4th and Pine, and was having itself a fine old time punching its way through the mannequins in the building. Well, these takers found that robot and cooked up a crazy plan to keep it busy feedin’ it dummies while one of their dummies crawled under it and turned it off. Damned lucky they didn’t get themselves killed, but I swear to god they pulled it off!

    Of course, all the commotion caught the attention of some casualties in the building, and while the takers were trying to finish them off, one of those fool takers got himself thrown through the boarded-up doors and into 3rd Ave like he was at an old timey saloon fight!
